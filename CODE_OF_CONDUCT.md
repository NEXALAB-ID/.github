# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers of NEXALAB ID pledge to make participation in our community a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, background, experience level, nationality, or field of expertise.

---

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Give and receive feedback graciously
- Stay on topic — keep discussions relevant to electronics, IoT, AI, and related fields
- Share knowledge openly and help others learn
- Credit others' work appropriately

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Discrimination based on age, background, experience level, or nationality
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Deliberately spreading misinformation
- Any behavior that creates a hostile or unwelcoming environment

---

## Our Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate action in response to any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all NEXALAB ID spaces — including all repositories, Discussions, Pull Requests, Issues, and any other community channels associated with NEXALAB ID.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a discussion at [NEXALAB ID Discussions](https://github.com/NEXALAB-ID/discussions/discussions). All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct may face consequences as determined by other members of the organization's leadership.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

| Violation | Consequence |
|:----------|:------------|
| Minor — inappropriate language or behavior | Written warning |
| Moderate — repeated violations or harassment | Temporary suspension from community spaces |
| Severe — serious harassment or targeted attacks | Permanent ban from all NEXALAB ID spaces |
